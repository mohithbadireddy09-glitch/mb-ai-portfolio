# AI Research Assistant

This project demonstrates how AI can automate business and market research workflows.

It uses structured prompts to generate organized research reports for business analysis.

## Workflow

1. Enter a research topic
2. Provide context (industry, region, objective)
3. AI generates a structured research report
4. Insights can be used for strategy or market analysis

## Tools Used

- ChatGPT
- Prompt engineering
- Structured reporting

## Example Prompt

Act as a business research analyst.

Research the following topic and produce a structured report.

Topic: [Enter topic]

Context:
- Industry
- Target region
- Business objective

Include analysis of:

- Key companies and competitors
- Market trends
- Opportunities and risks
- Strategic insights
- Future outlook

Output format:

1. Executive summary
2. Key insights
3. Bullet-point analysis
4. Strategic recommendation
