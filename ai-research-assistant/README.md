# AI Research Assistant

## Purpose
This project demonstrates how AI can assist in business research tasks.

## Problem
Businesses often spend hours researching competitors, markets, and trends.

## AI Workflow
1. User asks for research topic
2. AI gathers information
3. AI summarizes findings
4. AI creates structured report

## Tools
ChatGPT
Web research
Prompt engineering

## Example Use Case
Research the top 10 AI recruitment tools used by HR teams.
