# AI Content Generator

## Purpose
This project shows how AI can help businesses generate marketing and social media content quickly.

## Problem
Companies need consistent content for social media, blogs, and marketing campaigns.

## AI Workflow
1. User enters topic or business niche
2. AI generates structured content
3. AI suggests headlines and improvements
4. Final content prepared for publishing

## Tools Used
ChatGPT
Prompt frameworks
Content structure templates

## Example Use Case
Generate 5 LinkedIn posts for a recruitment consultancy about hiring trends.
